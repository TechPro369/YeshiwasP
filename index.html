About me.

A little bit about who I am

I'm Yeshiwas — a Computer Engineering student based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I love figuring out how things work, whether that's a piece of hardware, a tricky algorithm, or a clean piece of UI.

My path here wasn't a straight line. I started at AAU in 2022 as an Electrical Engineering major, but the university requires every engineering student to complete a broad freshman year before being assigned to a specific track. After that first year I was placed in Computer Engineering — a switch that turned out to fit me perfectly, sitting right where hardware meets software.

I treat my coursework as fuel for building things outside of class — small tools, experiments, and side projects that keep me sharp.

Outside of code, I'm usually reading, learning a new skill, or hanging out with friends and family.
